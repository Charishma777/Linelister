About us

This Linelister website specifically for working employees involves creating a platform that helps individuals efficiently manage their tasks, deadlines, and priorities in a professional environment Secure login system for employees to access their tasks. Role-based access control to manage permissions for different user roles. Ability to create, update, prioritize, and delete tasks. Option to categorize tasks by projects, departments, or priority levels. Setting due dates and reminders for tasks. Attachments and comments for additional context and collaboration.
